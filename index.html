About NAVAKSHARA

NAVAKSHARA is the premier aerospace techfest organized by the Aeromodeling Club of Centurion University of Technology and Management, Bhubaneswar. Our mission is to bring together the brightest minds to explore, innovate, and excel in the field of aerospace technology.

This year, we present three exhilarating competitions: RC Plane, Drone Racing, and Robot War. Join us for three days of innovation, competition, and aerospace excellence.
